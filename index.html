Join us at our next meeting!

Club meetings are held on the 2nd Tuesday of the month at 7:30 pm at the Spain Inn in Piscataway from April through October. Our next meeting is Tuesday April 12th, 2016. The program for the evening will feature the diving at Raja Ampat in Indonesia as an enticing preview of the Dive Club's proposed trip there in 2017.

Who we are

We are a group of central New Jersey recreational divers representing all ages, certifications, diving interests and levels of experience. Need a dive buddy? Join the club!

Activities

We organize boat dives, shore dives, dive vacations, picnics, a rafting trip, an annual holiday party and other social events. Please check out our calendar of activities.

Volunteer Efforts

Explorers Dive Club members provide volunteer support for visitor registration at the annual Beneath the Sea consumer and dive travel expo. We participate in beach cleanups and other activities to support the communities where we live.
